Language connects (or separates?) the top 50 LSPs

{{meta}}

Is translation destined to become a high-tech commodity, or will it evolve as a high value generator? This question is one of the industry’s defining narratives today, so I was curious to see how the world’s top LSPs position themselves on the market. I analyzed the language from these companies’ home pages and built the interactive visualization below.
I’ll let you explore and leave the conclusions to you. What patterns do you find? Do they match up with your own impression of these companies? If you’re one of the LSPs: does the language match with your own market positioning? I’m excited to hear.
A more detailed explanation of the methodology, the source data, and the code to reproduce, follows below the interactive part.
